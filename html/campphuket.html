Camp Phuket

Choose Camp Phuket for an incredible adventure on Thailand’s beautiful islands!
Camp Phuket is the ultimate Camp location, with elephant conservation, school visits, trips to Koh Phi Phi, Monkey Beach, Railay Beach and Krabi; with so many fun activities, it really has got everything you are looking for!
Camp Phuket combines exploring Thailand’s incredible islands with cultural experiences and so much more. With Camp Thailand, you will make friends for life and unforgettable memories, whilst exploring this beautiful country.
Not only will you get to explore the beautiful islands of Thailand, but you will be making a real difference to local communities while doing so. At Camp Phuket, you can marvel the sunset during BBQ’s on the beach, experience fire shows and take a dip in the crystal pool. You really will work hard, party hard and make a difference! What more could you want?
