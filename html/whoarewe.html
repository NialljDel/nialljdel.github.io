Who are we?

Invasion Camp Group is a business based in Salford with camps all around the world for both Under 18s and over 18s respectively. Our Camp Thailand program has seen success in the past few years, becoming our most successful program to date. We strive to give people from all walks of life the once in a lifetime experience to travel, educate others, volunteer, and culture themselves with the hope that it benefits them in later life. We believe that we can help to improve your skills and expand your knowledge to make you a better person through the program.



Our beliefs

Here at Invasion, we think that travel broadens the mind. By providing this opportunity to as many people as possible, we can create a better culture around us by inheriting parts of other cultures into ours. Through giving our time and care to the less fortunate in other countries, we think we can build not only a better, brighter world, but also a new generation of kind, helping people that will go on to make great changes in other people's lives.
